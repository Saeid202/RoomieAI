# Roomie AI Mortgage System - Complete Introduction

## Overview

Roomie AI's Mortgage System is a comprehensive digital mortgage application platform that connects home buyers with qualified mortgage brokers. The system streamlines the entire mortgage pre-qualification process, from initial application to broker matching and approval.

---

## 🎯 What Problem Does It Solve?

Traditional mortgage applications are:
- **Time-consuming**: Multiple meetings, paperwork, back-and-forth
- **Fragmented**: Documents scattered across emails, faxes, physical files
- **Opaque**: Buyers don't know their application status
- **Inefficient**: Brokers manually review each application

Roomie AI solves this by providing a **centralized, digital, transparent mortgage application platform**.

## 🏠 For Home Buyers - How It Works

### Step 1: Create Mortgage Profile

Buyers complete a comprehensive mortgage application with **6 main sections**:

#### 1. **Basic Information**
- Full name (first, middle, last)
- Email address
- Phone number
- Date of birth
- Current address
- Social Insurance Number (SIN)
- Marital status (single, married, common-law, divorced, widowed)

#### 2. **Employment & Income**
- Employment status (employed, self-employed, unemployed, retired, student)
- Employer name
- Job title
- Years at current job
- Employment type (full-time, part-time, contract, seasonal)
- Annual gross income
- Additional income sources (bonuses, commissions, rental income, investments)
- Income verification documents (T4s, pay stubs, NOAs)

#### 3. **Assets & Down Payment**
- Total savings amount
- Down payment amount available
- Down payment source (savings, gift, RRSP, inheritance, sale of property)
- Asset breakdown:
  - Bank accounts (chequing, savings)
  - Investments (RRSP, TFSA, stocks, bonds)
  - Real estate owned
  - Vehicles
  - Other assets

#### 4. **Credit & Debts**
- Credit score (self-reported or estimated)
- Credit history length
- Current debts:
  - Credit cards (balance, limit, monthly payment)
  - Car loans (balance, monthly payment)
  - Student loans (balance, monthly payment)
  - Personal loans (balance, monthly payment)
  - Other debts
- Total monthly debt payments
- Bankruptcy history (if any)
- Consumer proposal history (if any)

#### 5. **Property Preferences**
- Property type (detached, semi-detached, townhouse, condo, multi-unit)
- Preferred location (city, neighborhood)
- Price range (min-max)
- Number of bedrooms
- Number of bathrooms
- Property purpose (primary residence, investment, vacation home)
- Timeline to purchase (immediately, 1-3 months, 3-6 months, 6-12 months, 1+ years)

#### 6. **Mortgage Preferences**
- Mortgage amount needed
- Preferred mortgage term (1, 2, 3, 4, 5 years, or variable)
- Preferred amortization period (15, 20, 25, 30 years)
- Interest rate type preference (fixed, variable, hybrid)
- Pre-approval status (yes/no)
- First-time home buyer (yes/no)

### Step 2: Upload Documents

Buyers securely upload required mortgage documents:

**Required Documents:**
- Government-issued ID (driver's license, passport)
- Proof of income:
  - Last 2 years T4 slips
  - Last 2 years Notice of Assessment (NOA)
  - Recent pay stubs (last 3 months)
  - Employment letter
- Proof of down payment:
  - Bank statements (last 90 days)
  - Investment statements
  - Gift letter (if applicable)
- Credit documents:
  - Credit report (optional - broker can pull)
- Property documents (if applicable):
  - Purchase agreement
  - Property listing
  - Home inspection report

**Document Features:**
- Secure encrypted storage
- Automatic document categorization
- Expiry date tracking
- Version control
- Broker access control

### Step 3: Broker Consent & Matching

**Broker Consent System:**
- Buyers control which brokers can view their profile
- Toggle consent on/off for each broker
- Consent can be granted/revoked anytime
- Only consenting profiles are visible to brokers

**How Matching Works:**
- Buyers browse broker profiles (rates, specialties, reviews)
- Buyers grant consent to brokers they're interested in
- Brokers see only profiles where consent is granted
- Brokers can filter by location, property type, income level

### Step 4: Broker Review & Feedback

Once consent is granted:
- Broker reviews complete mortgage profile
- Broker accesses uploaded documents
- Broker provides feedback through platform:
  - Pre-approval likelihood
  - Estimated rates
  - Required additional documents
  - Next steps
  - Timeline expectations

### Step 5: Communication & Updates

**Feedback System:**
- Brokers send messages through platform
- Buyers receive notifications
- Two-way communication
- Document requests
- Status updates
- Meeting scheduling

### Step 6: Pre-Approval & Next Steps

- Broker provides pre-approval letter (if approved)
- Buyer downloads pre-approval
- Buyer can shop for properties with confidence
- Ongoing communication through platform

## 💼 For Mortgage Brokers - How It Works

### Step 1: Create Broker Profile

Brokers set up their professional profile:

**Profile Information:**
- Broker name and photo
- Brokerage name
- License number
- Years of experience
- Specialties (first-time buyers, self-employed, investment properties, etc.)
- Service areas (cities/regions served)
- Languages spoken
- Contact information
- Bio/description

**Rate Information:**
- Current mortgage rates (fixed/variable)
- Special offers
- Rate comparison

**Credentials:**
- Licenses and certifications
- Professional associations
- Awards and recognition

### Step 2: Access Client Dashboard

Brokers get a dedicated dashboard showing:

**Clients Tab:**
- List of all clients who granted consent
- Filter by:
  - Application status (new, in-review, pre-approved, declined)
  - Property type
  - Location
  - Income level
  - Down payment amount
  - Timeline
- Sort by date, income, property value

**Client Cards Display:**
- Client name
- Property preferences (type, location, price range)
- Income level
- Down payment amount
- Credit score estimate
- Timeline to purchase
- Application completeness (%)
- Last activity date

### Step 3: Review Client Applications

Click on a client to view:

**Profile Tab:**
- Complete mortgage application
- All 6 sections of information
- Application completeness indicator
- Last updated timestamp

**Documents Tab:**
- All uploaded documents organized by category
- Document viewer (secure, watermarked)
- Download documents (with audit trail)
- Request additional documents

**Feedback Tab:**
- Send feedback to client

- Communication log

### Step 4: Provide Feedback

Brokers can send structured feedback:

**Feedback Form:**

- Maximum mortgage amount
- Required additional documents
- Concerns or issues
- Next steps
- Timeline
- Personal message

**Feedback Features:**
- Rich text editor
- Attach documents
- Set follow-up reminders
- Mark as urgent
- Email notification to client

### Step 5: Manage Pipeline

**Dashboard Analytics:**
- Total clients
- New applications this week/month
- Pre-approvals issued
- Conversion rate

- Revenue pipeline

**Task Management:**
- Follow-up reminders
