If Your Role is NOT "landlord"

This is why you can't access the Applications page!

The RouteGuard component is blocking you because you don't have the landlord role.

Solution Options:

  1. Create a new landlord account:
    • Log out
    • Sign up with a new email
    • Select "Landlord" as your role during signup
  2. Change your existing account role (requires database access):
    • Go to Supabase Dashboard
    • Open SQL Editor
    • Run: UPDATE auth.users SET raw_user_meta_data = jsonb_set(raw_user_meta_data, '{role}', '"landlord"') WHERE email = 'your@email.com';
    • Log out and log back in
