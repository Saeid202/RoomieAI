# Roomie AI - Comprehensive Platform Introduction

## What is Roomie AI?

Roomie AI is Canada's most comprehensive AI-powered real estate platform that revolutionizes how people rent, buy, sell, and manage properties. We combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence with practical real estate solutions to serve renters, landlords, buyers, sellers, mortgage brokers, and real estate professionals.

---

## 🏠 Core Platform Features

### 1. **Rental Marketplace**

#### For Tenants/Seekers:
- **Smart Property Search**: Browse rental listings with advanced filters (location, price, amenities, pet-friendly, etc.)
- **Quick Apply**: One-click application system with pre-filled tenant profiles
- **Document Management**: Secure upload and storage of rental documents (ID, pay stubs, references)
- **Viewing Scheduler**: Calendly-style booking system to schedule property viewings with landlords
- **AI Property Assistant**: Chat with AI about property details, ask questions about listings, get instant answers
- **Roommate Matching**: Find compatible roommates based on lifestyle preferences and schedules
- **Opposite Schedule Matching**: Connect with roommates who have complementary work schedules
- **Application Tracking**: Monitor all your rental applications in one dashboard

#### For Landlords:
- **Property Listing Management**: Create, edit, and manage rental listings with photos and videos
- **Tenant Screening**: Review applications, documents, and tenant profiles
- **Viewing Appointment Management**: Set weekly availability, manage viewing requests, confirm/decline appointments
- **Property Categorization**: Organize properties by type (residential, commercial, student housing, etc.)
- **Document Vault**: Secure storage for property documents with controlled tenant access
- **Rent Collection**: Integrated payment processing with Stripe (credit/debit cards and PAD - Pre-Authorized Debit)
- **Payout Management**: Set up bank accounts or debit cards for automatic rent payouts
- **Lease Management**: Generate and manage Ontario Standard Lease Forms (2229E)
- **Property Archiving**: Archive inactive listings while preserving data
